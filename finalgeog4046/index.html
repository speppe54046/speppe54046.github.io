Map of East Baton Rouge Parks and Surrounding Demographics







This is a multilayered map that shows the classification, surrounding demographics, and the number of facilities of BREC Parks in East Baton Rouge Parish. This map was created as a final project for GEOG 4046 at Louisiana State University and its main purpose is to investigate the discrepancies between various BREC Parks by providing insight into the surrounding area and the parks themselves.
Though we are able to visually see differences in education, environmental, social & economic opportunities surrounding each of the BREC parks, a field survey for all parks is required in order to conclude whether or not the provided information is represented accurately and to update any outdated information. Improvement projects presented by BREC would also require further field surveying and investigation on where the funding is being provided and analysis of the results of the improvement project. Recommendations for areas that require improvement projects can not be concluded due to the lack of field surveys for this study.
Originally this project was designed to follow a story based outline, but due to the number of parks and the amount of information, it seemed more practical to let the user freely investigate rather than have a structured application with preset locations.

The toolbar above the map allows enables the user to toggle the legend and layers associated with this project. There is also the option to change the base-map. Zoom in or out as needed.
