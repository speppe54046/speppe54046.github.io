A map of Turkmenistan's Capital

This is a map of Turkmenistan's capitol Ashgabat. I have placed a marker on the Alem Center, a polygon around Ashgabat Stadium, and a circle around the Yyldz Hotel, representing three significant places in Ashgabat. The Alem Center is known for having the world's largest indoor ferris wheel and the Ashgabat Stadium is known for holding the world's largest cycling awareness lesson. The Yyldz Hotel is one of the best hotels Ashgabat and offers one of the best views of Ashgabat along with a 5 star service not found anywhere else. The polyline represents a 4.5 mile route between the Alem Center and the Ashbgabat Arena.
